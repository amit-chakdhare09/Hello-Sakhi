About Us

Our Mission

Hello Sakhi is dedicated to empowering women by providing a safe, accessible, and confidential platform to report cases of sexual and domestic assault. Our mission is to:
  • Educate and empower : We provide comprehensive information about women's rights, legal procedures, and available resources to help women understand their options and make informed decisions.

  • Create a supportive community : We foster a supportive community where women can connect with others who have experienced similar trauma and find encouragement and solidarity.

Our Approach

We believe that every woman deserves to feel safe and respected. Our approach is grounded in the following principles:
  • Confidentiality : We prioritize the privacy and confidentiality of every woman who comes to us for help. All information shared with us is treated with the utmost discretion.

  • Accessibility : We aim to make our services accessible to women from all walks of life, regardless of their socioeconomic status, location, or language preferences.

  • Compassion and support : We offer compassionate and supportive services to women throughout their journey, providing guidance, counseling, and assistance as needed.

Join Us in the Fight for Justice

By supporting Hello Sakhi, you are helping to create a safer and more just world for women. Together, we can break the cycle of violence and empower women to reclaim their lives.
